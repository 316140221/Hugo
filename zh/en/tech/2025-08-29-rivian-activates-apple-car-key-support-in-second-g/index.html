Rivian Activates Apple Car Key Support in Second-Generation Vehicles

发布时间: 2025-08-29 01:54 | 分类: tech | 标签: iPhone Car Keys Rivian

EV manufacturer Rivian appears to finally be rolling out support for Apple’s digital car keys available through the Wallet app on iPhone and Apple Watch. Rivian announced support for the feature in debuting its second-generation R1S and R1T over a year ago, but the company had not activated it until today, as spotted in an Apple configuration file by MacRumors contributor Aaron Perris. While we can’t confirm whether it’s up and running for all users just yet, Apple has pushed support live on its end, so owners of supported Rivian vehicles should hopefully be seeing it soon.
