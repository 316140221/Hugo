‘Adolescence’ Star Stephen Graham Wins Limited Series Actor Emmy: This ‘Doesn’t Normally Happen’ to a ‘Mixed Race Kid’ Like Me

发布时间: 2025-09-15 02:49 | 分类: entertainment | 标签: News Adolescence Emmys

Stephen Graham won the lead limited series actor Emmy on Sunday, recognizing his portrayal of Eddie Miller in Netflix’s “Adolescence,” which he co-created. “This kind of thing doesn’t normally happen to a kid like me. I’m just a mixed-race kid from a block of flats in a place called Kirkby,” said Graham, who is of Swedish and Jamaican descent. […]
