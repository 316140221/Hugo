New Report Lists RAM Amounts in iPhone 17 and iPhone 17 Pro Models

发布时间: 2025-09-04 13:10 | 分类: tech | 标签: iPhone 17 iPhone 17 Air iPhone 17 Pro TrendForce

Three out of four iPhone 17 models will feature more RAM than the equivalent iPhone 16 models, according to a new report that aligns with previous rumors. The all-new iPhone 17 Air, the iPhone 17 Pro, and the iPhone 17 Pro Max will each be equipped with 12GB of RAM, according to Taiwanese market intelligence firm TrendForce, which cited “current market information and analyst projections.” The base model iPhone 17 will have 8GB of RAM, matching the amount included in all iPhone 16 models, according to TrendForce.
