AirPods Pro 3 Use Acoustic Seal Test for Optimizing Sound Quality and ANC

发布时间: 2025-09-16 23:37 | 分类: tech | 标签: AirPods Pro Featured

The AirPods Pro 3 use an Acoustic Seal Test to check the fit of ear tips, according to a new Apple support document. Apple says that the Acoustic Seal Test will ensure that you’re using the proper ear tip size for the best sound quality and noise cancellation. The AirPods Pro 1 and the AirPods Pro 2 have an Ear Tip Fit Test, but Apple updated the process for the ‌AirPods Pro 3‌. Apple suggests starting with the medium size ear tip. If you can’t achieve a good seal, switch to the larger tip. If the ear tip feels too large, switch to a smaller tip. Apple designed new foam-infused silicone ear tips for the ‌AirPods Pro 3‌, which it says are softer and less stiff than the AirPods Pro 2 ear tips. ‌AirPods Pro 3‌ ear tips come in five sizes, including XXS, XS, S, M, and L.
