Italian distribution company I Wonder Pictures has acquired rights to Venice-bound “Broken English,” a portrait of the late Marianne Faithfull directed by BAFTA nominees Iain Forsyth and Jane Pollard (“20,000 Days on Earth”). “Broken English” is set to world premiere in the Out of Competition section at the Venice Film Festival on Aug. 30.
