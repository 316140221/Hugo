Polly Holliday, a prolific stage and screen actress known for playing the gum-smacking waitress Flo who popularized the phrase &#8220;Kiss my grits!&#8221; on the long-running CBS sitcom &#8220;Alice,&#8221; died Tuesday at her home in Manhattan. She was 88. Holliday&#8217;s death was confirmed to the New York Times by her longtime agent and friend Dennis Aspland. [&#8230;]
