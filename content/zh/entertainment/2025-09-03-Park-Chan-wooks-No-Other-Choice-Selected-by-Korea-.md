The Korean Film Council has selected Park Chan-wook’s &#8220;No Other Choice&#8221; as the country’s submission for best international feature at the 98th Academy Awards. The move positions the acclaimed filmmaker — whose credits include &#8220;Oldboy,&#8221; &#8220;The Handmaiden&#8221; and &#8220;Decision to Leave,&#8221; which was shortlisted in the category, — for a fresh run at Oscar glory.
