Variety will publish its fourth annual 10 Artisans to Watch list on Oct. 22 and recognize the honorees at this year’s SCAD Savannah Film Festival. As Variety continues to elevate artisans in its coverage, the 10 selected will be spotlighted in a panel conversation at the festival moderated by Variety senior artisans editor Jazz Tangcay.
