Greg Louganis has signed with Innovative Artists Entertainment (IAE) for representation, Variety has learned exclusively. IAE will rep Louganis in television hosting, commentary, brand endorsements, speaking engagements, and podcast development. The world-renowned diver continues to be represented by Ameri Law. Louganis is a highly-decorated Olympic diver. He won four Olympic gold medals in his career [&#8230;]
