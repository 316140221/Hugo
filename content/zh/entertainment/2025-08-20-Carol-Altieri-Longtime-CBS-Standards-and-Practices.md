Longtime CBS standards and practices executive Carol Altieri has died at her home in Palm Desert, Calif. after a long illness. She was 76. Altieri died Aug. 9, CBS confirmed. A native of Queens who grew up on Long Island, Altieri spent 35 years at CBS, rising through the ranks of its program practices department. [&#8230;]
