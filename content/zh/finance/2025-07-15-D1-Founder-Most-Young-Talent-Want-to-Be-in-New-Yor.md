D1 Capital Partners Founder and Chief Investment Officer Dan Sundheim tells David Rubenstein that though his hedge fund is co-headquartered in Miami and New York, 75% of younger talent prefers to be based in New York City. Sundheim spoke in an interview for "Bloomberg Wealth with David Rubenstein." This interview was recorded May 27 in New York. (Source: Bloomberg)
