ANZ Head of FX Research Mahjabeen Zaman sees a push-pull in market expectations between a 25bps and 50bps cut at the Fed's September meeting. She also sees a weaker dollar but says the large move down has taken place in the first half of the year. She speaks to Bloomberg's Lizzy Burden on 'Horizons Middle East and Africa.'
