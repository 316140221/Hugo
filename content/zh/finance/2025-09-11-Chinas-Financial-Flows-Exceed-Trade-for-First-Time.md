For the first time in China, the amount of money flowing in search of investment opportunities is more than the value of goods and services its trade engines generate. The historic shift moves China a small step closer to counterparts like the US and Japan. Tian Chen reports on "Bloomberg: The China Show." (Source: Bloomberg)
