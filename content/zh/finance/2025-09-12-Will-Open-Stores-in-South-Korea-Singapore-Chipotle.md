Chipotle Mexican Grill Inc. is venturing into Asia for the first time, with plans to open locations in South Korea and Singapore in 2026. Chief Executive Officer Scott Boatwright tells Romaine Bostick and Katie Greifeld on "The Close" he expects international markets to become the next layer of growth beyond North America that'll support the brand in the long term. (Source: Bloomberg)
