Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank, ADIB, reported net income for the second quarter of AED 1.78 billion up 13% Y/Y. The lender also had a strong first half, with net income climbing 15% in comparison to 2024. Mohamed Abdelbary, Group CEO of ADIB spoke to Horizons Middle East and Africa anchor Joumanna Bercetche on the numbers & the reaction to the geopolitical volatility in markets. (Source: Bloomberg)
