President Donald Trump expects to announce economic deals totaling more than $10 billion during his trip to the United Kingdom, according to senior US officials. Some of the deals are expected to be rolled out at a business reception hosted by Chancellor of the Exchequer Rachel Reeves and featuring both Trump and British Prime Minister Keir Starmer. Bloomberg’s Rosalind Mathieson reports.
