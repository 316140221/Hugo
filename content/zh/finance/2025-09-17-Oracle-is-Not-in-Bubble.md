Oracle is part of the consortium set to keep TikTok running in the US. The stock has been soaring on earnings and AI deals. It's risen so fast that some analysts are warning of a bubble. But Jefferies’ Brent Thill isn’t worried. He has a buy rating on Oracle with a $360 price target. He joined Bloomberg Open Interest with Matt Miller and Dani Burger. (Source: Bloomberg)
