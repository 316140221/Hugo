Sean Darby of Mizuho Securities says President Trump's tax and spending bill "encouraged front-loading of investment, particularly into data centers and also into tech equipment." According to Darby, Asian companies will get an added boost from those measures coming through, which means "relatively good news for the tech industry in Taiwan and Korea." (Source: Bloomberg)
