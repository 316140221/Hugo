Heriyanto Irawan, member of Indonesia’s National Economic Council, says securing a 19% tariff rate with the US is a “huge win” for some of the nation’s most labor-intensive sectors. Speaking on Bloomberg's Insight with Haslinda Amin, he said the deal opens up opportunity for Indonesia to get additional market into the US. (Source: Bloomberg)
