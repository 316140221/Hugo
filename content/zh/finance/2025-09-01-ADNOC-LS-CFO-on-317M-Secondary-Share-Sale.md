Abu Dhabi’s state energy company completed a $317 million placement to institutional investors of approximately 222 million shares in ADNOC Logistics and Services. CFO Nicholas Gleeson speaks to Bloomberg's Horizons Middle East and Africa anchor Joumanna Bercetche about the share sale and a potential MSCI inclusion, investors' demand, the outlook for the company and signs of growth across the region.
