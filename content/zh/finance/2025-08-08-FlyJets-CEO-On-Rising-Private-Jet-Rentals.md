Jessica Fisher, FlyJets CEO and Founder, describes FlyJets as an Airbnb for private jet bookings, where an operator or owner can list their plane and someone can book it. The company updates aircraft listings every five minutes and allows automatic booking. She speaks with Scarlet Fu and Katie Greifeld on “The Close.” (Source: Bloomberg)
