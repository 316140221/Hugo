For the third year in a row, Dubai has topped the ranking as the world's best destination for executive nomads, with Abu Dhabi hot on its heels taking second place for the second year in a row. A new report from Savills highlights how the UAE continues to attract mobile professionals with its world-class infrastructure, connectivity, and unbeatable lifestyle advantages. In an exclusive interview, Andrew Cummings, Residential Agency Head, Middle East at Savills told Bloomberg’s Horizons Middle East and Africa anchor Joumanna Bercetche about the criteria that ranked Dubai and Abu Dhabi high on the list. (Source: Bloomberg)
