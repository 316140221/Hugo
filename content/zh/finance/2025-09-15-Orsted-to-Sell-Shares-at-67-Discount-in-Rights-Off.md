Orsted A/S plans to sell new shares at 67% below Friday’s close as the offshore wind developer works to rebuild investor confidence after a bet on the US market went wrong. The success of the 60 billion Danish kroner ($9.4 billion) rights offering &mdash; the biggest for a European energy company in over a decade &mdash; is critical for Orsted to be able to finally turn the page on a crisis that has lasted several years. Bloomberg’s Christian Wienberg reports.
