Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell underscored the need for the banking system’s capital framework to work together effectively, adding that competition is also crucial for the banking industry. Powell delivered introductory remarks Tuesday at the Fed’s “Integrated Review of the Capital Framework for Large Banks Conference.”
