US Food and Drug Administration Commissioner Marty Makary said the federal government has not yet started writing a report on the possible causes of autism but reaffirmed a longstanding promise that it will be released “within a month.” He says it's premature to assume that Tylenol could be a root cause. Dr. Makary says they're "looking at everything" and you're going to see some "amazing things" in this new report. (Source: Bloomberg)
