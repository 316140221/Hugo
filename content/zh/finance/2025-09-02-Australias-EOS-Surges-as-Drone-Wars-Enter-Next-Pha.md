Global defense spending is surging and Australia's Electro Optic Systems is cashing in. The company, which supplies lasers and counter-drone systems to clients across the US, Australia, and the Gulf, just landed a €71M NATO drone-defense deal. EOS CEO Andreas Schwer spoke exclusively on "Bloomberg: The Asia Trade" about the prospects for long-term growth. (Source: Bloomberg)
