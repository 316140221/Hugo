Oracle is in discussions with Meta Platforms for a cloud computing deal worth about $20 billion. Under the multiyear deal, Oracle would provide the social media giant with computing power for training and deploying artificial intelligence models, according to people familiar with the talks. Brody Ford has more on “Bloomberg The Close.”
