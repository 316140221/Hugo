European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen met with US President Donald Trump at Trump Turnberry golf club in Scotland on July 27. The leaders agreed to a deal that will see the bloc face 15% tariffs on most of its exports, including automobiles, staving off a trade war that could have delivered a hammer blow to the global economy.
