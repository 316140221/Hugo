Christopher Waller is the best option out of the contenders for next Fed chair says Richard Portes, Professor of Economics at the London Business School and founder of the Centre for Economic Policy Research. Kevin Hassett, meanwhile, would be a 'disaster' warns Portes. Speaking to Bloomberg's Francine Lacqua, Portes added that 'anybody who Trump puts up will be confirmed'. (Source: Bloomberg)
