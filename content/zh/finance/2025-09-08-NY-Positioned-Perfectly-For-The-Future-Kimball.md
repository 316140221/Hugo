Andrew Kimball, CEO of the New York City Economic Development Corp., says there’s no question about a job slowdown in New York, but the fundamentals remain strong with record private-sector and total jobs. He tells Romaine Bostick and Katie Greifeld on “The Close” the city lost 1 million jobs during COVID but has since regained them. (Source: Bloomberg)
