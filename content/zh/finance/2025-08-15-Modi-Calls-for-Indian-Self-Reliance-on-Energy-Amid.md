Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi talked about the rise of "economic selfishness" and reiterated how his country needs to be self-reliant on energy, during an Independence Day speech. This comes in the wake of US President Donald Trump doubling tariffs on Indian goods to 50% as penalty for its purchases of Russian oil. (Source: PIB)
