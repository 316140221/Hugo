The S&amp;P 500 Index hit an intraday record after a weaker-than-expected payrolls report bolstered expectations for a Federal Reserve interest-rate cut later this month. A rally for Broadcom Inc. led tech and chip stocks higher, while a decline in 10-year Treasury yields to 4.07% in the wake of the jobs data also helped equities.
