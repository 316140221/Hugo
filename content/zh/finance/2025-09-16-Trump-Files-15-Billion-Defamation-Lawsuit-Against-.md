President Donald Trump filed a $15 billion defamation lawsuit against The New York Times, accusing the paper of serving as a “mouthpiece” for the Democrats. Trump made the announcement on Truth Social. The Times says the lawsuit "has no merit." Bloomberg's Suzanne Lynch reports. (Source: Bloomberg)
