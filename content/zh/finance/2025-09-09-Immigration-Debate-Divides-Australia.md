Australia's long-standing debate over immigration has been reignited recently, after anti-immigration protests swept across major cities, some hijacked by Neo-Nazi voices. Those protests were met with counter-demonstrations and Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has called for calm. University of Sydney Associate Professor Anna Boucher talks about how the rallies have exposed deep divisions in the country on "Bloomberg: The Asia Trade." (Source: Bloomberg)
