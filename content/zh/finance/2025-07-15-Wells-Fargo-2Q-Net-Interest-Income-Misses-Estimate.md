Wells Fargo &amp; Co. posted $11.7 billion in net interest income in the second quarter, falling just short of analysts’ estimates of $11.8 billion. That prompted Wells Fargo to lower its full-year NII growth target to little changed from last year, down from prior guidance of 1% to 3% growth. Sonali Basak reports on Bloomberg Television.
