Advanced Micro Devices Inc. CEO Lisa Su says she is seeing many positive signals for the business, even though the company failed to deliver a clear outlook for resuming sales in China. Su says the roadmap for the rest of the world is strong and "close conversations" are being held with the Trump administration so AMD can begin shipping popular AI processor chips to China again. She speaks on "Bloomberg Technology." (Source: Bloomberg)
