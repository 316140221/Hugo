Get a jump start on the US trading day with Matt Miller and Dani Burger on "Bloomberg Open Interest." Retail sales come in strong in August for the third straight month. Meanwhile, The SEC says it's prioritizing President Trump's push to reduce the frequency of quarterly reports.
