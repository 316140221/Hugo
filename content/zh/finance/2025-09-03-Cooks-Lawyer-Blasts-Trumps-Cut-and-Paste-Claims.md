The lawyer for Federal Reserve Governor Lisa Cook says President Donald Trump's moves to oust her are based on "cut-and-paste" allegations of mortgage fraud made in social media posts. Cook's attorney made a series of legal filings on Tuesday. Abbe Lowell says Cook "did not ever commit mortgage fraud." Bloomberg's Tyler Kendall reports. (Source: Bloomberg)
