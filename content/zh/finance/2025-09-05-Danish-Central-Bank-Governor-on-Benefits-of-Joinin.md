National Bank of Denmark Governor Christian Kettel Thomsen discusses the potential benefits of adopting the euro currency. During an exclusive conversation with Bloomberg’s Sanne Wass at the bank’s headquarters in Copenhagen, Kettel Thomsen says that being part of the single currency can potentially give governments, particularly those representing smaller nations, a stronger voice when it comes to European Union decisions.
