As rising tariffs push input costs higher, global music accessories manufacturer D’Addario is retooling its supply chain to protect its margins. The company has accelerated its onshoring efforts, opening domestic production lines and cutting reliance on China. With 95% of its products now made in the US, D’Addario sees new opportunity in US production even as global trade uncertainty continues to pressure exporters. (Source: Bloomberg)
