Prime Minister Narendra Modi used his first trip to China in seven years to reset relations with India’s powerful neighbor while also seeking to strengthen ties with Russia. This comes as the US imposed 50% tariffs on India, ramping up tensions between the two countries. Swasti Rao, Professor at O.P. Jindal Global University told Bloomberg’s Horizons Middle East and Africa anchor Joumanna Bercetche this meeting is a tactical way to manage complex relations. (Source: Bloomberg)
