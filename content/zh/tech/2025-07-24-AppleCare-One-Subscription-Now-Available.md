AppleCare One, Apple's new subscription for customers to cover multiple devices with a single plan, is now available. AppleCare One starts at &#36;19.99 per month for up to three products, with the ability to add more for &#36;5.99 per month for each additional device. The plan incudes all of the benefits that come with &zwnj;AppleCare&zwnj;+, such as unlimited repairs for accidents, priority support, battery coverage, and more. Subscribers can freely move their devices in and out of the plan at any time. &zwnj;AppleCare&zwnj; One also enables users to add products they already own that are up to four years old, providing they are in good condition, extending beyond the current 60-day window to purchase &zwnj;AppleCare&zwnj;+. When a customer trades in a covered product with Apple, it is automatically removed from their &zwnj;AppleCare&zwnj; One plan and replaced with the new device. &zwnj;AppleCare&zwnj; One also expands theft and loss protection to the iPad and Apple Watch.
