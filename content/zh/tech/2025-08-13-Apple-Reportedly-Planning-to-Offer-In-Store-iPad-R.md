The participating stores will be mostly located in the United States. The faulty or broken device is then sent to a repair center. The final invoice is apparently often revised down since the repair subcontractor only replaces the damaged part, but an accurate picture of the final repair price is not provided during the customer's first appointment at the Genius Bar.
