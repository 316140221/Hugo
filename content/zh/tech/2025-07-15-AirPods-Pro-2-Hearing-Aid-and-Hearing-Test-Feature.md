Apple is bringing the AirPods Pro 2 Hearing Tests and Hearing Aid functionality to multiple new countries in Europe, Asia, and South America. The &zwnj;AirPods Pro&zwnj; 2 hearing test mimics the hearing tests one might encounter at a doctor's office or when visiting an audiologist. Tones at different frequencies and sound levels play in each ear, with users instructed to tap the &zwnj;iPhone&zwnj;'s display whenever a sound is heard. Apple tests four frequencies, including 500Hz, 1kHz, 2kHz, and 4kHz.
