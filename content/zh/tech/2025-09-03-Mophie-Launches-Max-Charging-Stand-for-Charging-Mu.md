Mophie today announced the launch of the Max Charging Stand, a two-in-one charger that's designed for the USB-C AirPods Max and the AirPods or AirPods Pro. The Max Charging Stand holds the &zwnj;AirPods Max&zwnj; upright, with a magnetic USB-C charging dongle that connects to the USB-C port in the headphones. Mophie created the stand with the magnetic dongle to ensure that the &zwnj;AirPods Max&zwnj; are aligned perfectly for charging without the user having to fuss with positioning. There are also included magnets to ensure that the &zwnj;AirPods Max&zwnj; are put into low power mode while they're on the charger, increasing charger speed. Along with the upright charger for the &zwnj;AirPods Max&zwnj;, there is a smaller Qi-based charging platform that can be used for AirPods or &zwnj;AirPods Pro&zwnj; that support wireless charging.
