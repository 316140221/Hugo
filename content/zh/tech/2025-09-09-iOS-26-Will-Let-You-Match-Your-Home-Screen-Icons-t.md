The release candidate version of iOS 26 includes a new feature that lets you set an icon tint color that matches your iPhone case. When you long press on the Home Screen and then choose the customize option, there's a new &zwnj;iPhone&zwnj; case icon at the bottom of the "Tinted" menu. Tapping it will automatically change the color of &zwnj;Home Screen&zwnj; icons to the color of the case that you're using. Of course, this requires a MagSafe case that the &zwnj;iPhone&zwnj; recognizes, so it is essentially a feature designed to work with Apple cases. If you don't see a colorful &zwnj;MagSafe&zwnj; ring that matches your case color when you put your case on your &zwnj;iPhone&zwnj;, it's probably not going to work with icon tinting. Tinted icons in &zwnj;iOS 26&zwnj; have a new Liquid Glass look with added translucency. Tints are applied to all icons and widgets on the &zwnj;Home Screen&zwnj;.
