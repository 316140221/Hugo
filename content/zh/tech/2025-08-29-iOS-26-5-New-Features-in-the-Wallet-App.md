iOS 26 adds several features to the built-in Wallet app on the iPhone, providing a new way to track your online shopping, an update to boarding passes, and more. We've outlined everything new in the Wallet app below. Order Tracking The &zwnj;iOS 26&zwnj; Wallet app features enhanced order tracking, and you can now use it to track all of your purchases rather than just those made using Apple Pay. Apple Intelligence is used to locate order related information from the Mail app, and then orders are pulled into the Wallet app. The &zwnj;iPhone&zwnj; can detect tracking numbers and order numbers, and update the status of packages as they move from order to delivery. Orders can be accessed by tapping on the "..." button in the Wallet app and tapping on the Orders option. The feature is opt-in, and you do need a device capable of &zwnj;Apple Intelligence&zwnj; to use it.
