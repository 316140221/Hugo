Japanese band Kroi's latest music video for the "Method" single was recorded using 40 iPhone 16 Pro models, according to Apple. The iPhones were used to capture video from every angle simultaneously, with all of the shots merged into a single 3-minute music video. Apple shared details on Instagram, and Apple's marketing chief Greg Joswiak also highlighted the video on social media.
